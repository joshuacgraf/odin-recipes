Chorizo & Eggs over Mexican Rice

Description

An American's take on steak and eggs inspired by mexican ingredients and recipes making no claims to authenticity but hopefully tastiness. A bed of mexican rice coated by broken down chorizo sausage, over easy eggs, cojita cheese, onions and cilantro. For those who love spice, I invite you to join me in adding the green El Yucateco Salta Picante de Chile Habanero!

Ingredients

  • Mexican Rice
  • 6 oz chorizo
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 2 eggs
  • Cojita cheese
  • 1/4 thinly diced yellow onion
  • 1/4 cup sliced cilantro
  • Hot sauce of choice (optional)

Directions

  1. Make Mexican Rice.
  2. Add 1 tbsp oil to pan over medium high heat. Once warm, add chorizo. When cooked, remove and place over rice.
  3. Add 1 tbsp oil to pan over medium heat. Add eggs, cook as desired and place over rice and chorizo.
  4. Garnish with cojita cheese, hot sauce, onion, and cilantro as desired.
